Stylus

A universal digital ink canvas — pen, stylus, or finger. No install, no login.

Stylus is a distraction-free writing & drawing canvas that runs entirely in the browser on any device. Write or sketch with a pen, finger, or stylus; type text; then turn your handwriting into editable text with on-device OCR and polish it with Claude AI. Everything is saved locally — privacy-friendly, works offline. This guide walks through every button and panel.

★

Capabilities at a glance

✎Natural drawing

Pressure & tilt-aware ink with smooth curves. One pipeline for mouse, touch, pen and Apple Pencil; palm-rejection built in.

⌫Eraser & lasso

Erase strokes on contact, or lasso-select to move and delete groups of strokes.

TTyped text

Place, drag and colour text boxes; type via the on-screen keyboard, a physical keyboard, or paste.

▦Paper styles

Blank, Grid, Lined and Dot-grid backgrounds — cycle with one button.

✦AI handwriting → text

On-device OCR turns ink into editable text, then Claude polishes it six ways.

⤢Zoom & pan

Pinch / ⌘-scroll to zoom toward the cursor, scroll to pan, and a −/+/reset control cluster.

⇄Undo / Redo / Clear

Full history with keyboard shortcuts; Clear confirms before wiping the canvas.

⤓Export

Save your work as a PNG image or a PDF document.

⧉Multi-document

Unlimited local notes with rename & delete — all saved in-browser, no login.

⌨Hardware

Connect a Bluetooth stylus and a Scanmarker scanner (where the browser supports it).
